Our Story

Marwar's Finest Tradition

Laxmi Jewellers and Laxmi Fashion & Safa House Showroom is Siyawat's most trusted name in traditional Rajasthani fashion. From royal safas and sherwani to divine bhaghwan ki chadre, we bring heritage craftsmanship to every occasion.

Founded by Narsa Surana, our two-floor showroom on Sayla Bagoda Main Road has served thousands of families with pride, authenticity, and the blessings of Mata Ji.
